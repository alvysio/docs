title: "How to Change or Add a Contact on a Load"
description: "Change the customer contact on a load, either by picking an existing contact or by adding a new one that is saved back to the customer's profile."
---

<Note>
  **Applies to:** Dispatchers · Brokers · Accounting team members

  **Module:** Loads and Trips

  **Last reviewed:** 2026-07 · **Owner:** Support team

  Every load carries a **customer contact** — the person at the customer's company you coordinate with for that load. You can swap that contact for another one already on file, or add a brand-new contact from the load itself. New contacts you add here are also saved to the customer's profile so they are available on future loads.
</Note>

## Overview

The Load Details page shows the current customer contact for the load. Use the **Change Contact** flow when you need to:

- Point the load at a different person on the customer's team (for example, coverage during PTO).
- Add a new customer contact you have just been given, without leaving the load.
- Correct a contact that was set incorrectly when the load was created.

Two paths are available from the same modal:

- **Select an existing contact** — pick from contacts already saved on the customer's profile. Only the load is updated.
- **Add a new contact** — enter the new contact's details. The contact is saved to the customer's profile and assigned to the load in one step.

## Before You Start

- The load must have a **customer** assigned. If it does not, an error appears and you must set the customer first.
- The load does not need to be in any particular status to change the contact.
- You do not need the customer to have a **Physical Address** on file. Adding a new contact works for customers set up with a **billing/invoicing address only**.

## Change to an Existing Contact

Use this path when the person you need is already saved on the customer.

1. Open the load. You land on the Load Details page.
2. In the customer section, click **Change Contact**.
3. In the Change Contact modal, select the contact you want from the list.
4. Confirm the selection. The load's contact updates immediately; the customer's profile is unchanged.

## Add a New Contact from the Load

Use this path when the contact is not yet saved on the customer.

1. Open the load and click **Change Contact**.
2. In the Change Contact modal, choose to add a new contact and enter the contact's details (name, email, phone, and any other requested fields).
3. Save the new contact.

Alvys does two things on save:

- Adds the new contact to the **customer's profile**, so it is available for future loads.
- Assigns the new contact to **this load** as the current customer contact.

<Tip>
  You can add a new contact even if the customer has no Physical Address on file — for example, a billing-only customer set up with just an invoicing address. Both the profile update and the load-contact assignment still run.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title='"Cannot find customer to change contact" error'>
    This appears when the load has no customer assigned, or when the customer cannot be loaded. Set (or reselect) the customer on the load, then try **Change Contact** again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="New contact I just added is not on the load">
    If you added the contact from **Change Contact** on a load, the contact is saved to the customer and assigned to the load in the same step. Refresh the Load Details page — you should see the new contact selected. If it is still missing, confirm the customer on the load is the one you expected, and repeat the flow.

    <Note>
      Before July 16, 2026, adding a new contact from **Change Contact** could silently fail when the customer had no Physical Address (for example, billing-only customers). This has been fixed — adding a new contact now works for every customer, regardless of address setup.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
