## Support

The **Support** role consists of users who provide technical assistance to other users. They are responsible for troubleshooting, resolving technical issues, and ensuring the smooth operation of the platform for all users.

## Admin

The **Admin** role encompasses users who have full access to the system within their specific tenant. Often referred to as **Tenant Administrators**, these users are responsible for managing their organization's overall configuration and settings in Alvys.

## Partner Admin

The **Partner Admin** role is assigned to users who own the tenant account. They have the highest level of control over their tenant's account, including billing and account-level settings.

## Office Admin

Users with the **Office Admin** role oversee office-related configurations and resources. Their responsibilities include:

- Managing office-specific settings.
- Overseeing office users and their access levels.
- Ensuring efficient office operations within the system.

## Dispatcher

The **Dispatcher** role focuses on coordinating and managing driver activities. Dispatchers are responsible for:

- Assigning and managing tasks for drivers.
- Monitoring driver statuses and locations.
- Optimizing routes and schedules for efficiency.

## Biller

Users with the **Biller** role handle financial and invoicing tasks, including:

- Generating and managing invoices.
- Recording and overseeing payment transactions.
- Managing billing-related records and data

## Sales Agent

The **Sales Agent** role is tailored for users engaged in sales activities. Their duties include:

- Tracking leads and managing the sales pipeline.
- Building and maintaining customer relationships.
- Performing sales-related tasks to drive business growth.

## Data Entry

The **Data Entry** role grants users the ability to handle and maintain system data. Key responsibilities include:

- Entering and updating data accurately.
- Managing data records to ensure consistency.
- Supporting overall data integrity within the platform.

## Driver

The **Driver** role provides users with tools and features tailored to drivers. They can:

- View and update schedules.
- Log trips and manage their driving records.
- Access driver-specific functionalities for daily operations.

## Operations Manager

The **Operations Manager** role is designed for users overseeing operational activities. Their responsibilities include:

- Managing workflows and operational tasks.
- Monitoring performance metrics and KPIs.
- Ensuring smooth day-to-day operations within the system.

## Safety

The **Safety** role supports users in maintaining safety standards and compliance. Users with this role can:

- Monitor and enforce safety protocols.
- Conduct audits and inspections.
- Manage safety-related incidents and documentation

This structured role-based system ensures clarity, accountability, and security for all Alvys users
