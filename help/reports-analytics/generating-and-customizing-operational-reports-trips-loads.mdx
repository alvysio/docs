## Generating and Customizing Operational Reports - Trips &amp; Loads

Alvys's **Custom Reports** provide powerful tools to analyze your operational data, specifically for **Loads** and **Trips**. These reports are highly customizable, allowing you to filter by extensive criteria, dynamically manage columns, and even import data for deeper, tailored analysis.

**Summary:** Custom Reports enable you to generate detailed insights into your Load and Trip data. You can access both "Loads Custom Report" and "Trips Custom Report," apply a wide range of filters to narrow down your results, customize which columns appear in your report, and even import loads data.

---

### Using the Trips Custom Report

The "Trips Custom Report" allows you to analyze individual trip segments.

1. **Select "Trips Custom Report":**

   - From the "Operational Reports" section, click on **"Trips Custom Report."**
   - This will open the Trips Custom Report page.
2. **Apply Search Filters:**

   - You will see various filtering fields at the top of the page.
   - **Initial Filters **include:
   - Customer
   - Carrier
   - Driver
   - Load Office (0:24)
   - **More Search Criteria:** Click the **blue button** to expand the filtering options. (0:43)
   - Additional fields include:
   - Load Status
   - Ship Date
   - Delivery Date
   - Invoice Date
   - Release Date
   - Dispatcher Name
   - Truck
   - Trailer
   - Invoice As
   - Date Dispatched
   - Customer Sales Agent
   - **Include Historical Data:** You can also check the box to **"Include historical data."**
3. **Generate Report:**

   - Once your desired criteria are selected, click the **"Search"** button.
   - The table at the bottom half of the page will display the results that match your filtered search criteria.

     ![Notion Image](https://alvys.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/jmo4m9ce/2532418282/7d7ddd304966376350774a5f38ba/upload_2105478027863579457.png)
4. **Customize Columns:**

   - Similar to other tables in Alvys, you can:
   - Add new columns to your report.
   - Remove columns you don't need.
   - Rearrange the order of columns by dragging and dropping.
5. **Switch to Loads Data:**

   - You can easily tab between the "Trips" and "Loads" data from a tab located above the report table.

### Using the Loads Custom Report

The "Loads Custom Report" allows you to analyze full load records.

1. **Select "Loads Custom Report":**

   - From the "Operational Reports" section (under "Reports" in the side navigation), click on **"Loads Custom Report."**
   - This will open the Loads Custom Report page.
2. **Apply Filters:**

   - Similar to the Trips Custom Report, initial filtering fields include:
   - Customer
   - Carrier
   - Driver
   - Load Office
   - Click the **blue button** to add additional search criteria:
   - Load Status
   - Ship Date
   - Delivery Date
   - Invoice Date
   - Release Date
   - Dispatcher
   - Truck
   - Trailer
   - Invoice As
   - Dispatch Date
   - Customer Sales Agent (2:48)
   - You also have the option to **"Include historical data."**
3. **Generate Report:**

   - Click the **blue "Search"** button to generate the report.
   - A table will appear at the bottom with your custom report for the loads.
4. **Tabs for Report Views:**

   - Above the report table, you will find tabs for **"Loads," "Trips," and "Imported."**
5. **Import Data into Report (for analysis):**

   - You can click the **"Import"** button.
   - This allows you to upload a document (e.g., a spreadsheet) to getdata into your custom report for analysis.
   - You will be provided the option to download a "Sample Load Format"
   - You will be prompted to select a "Subsidiary" before uploading your document.

     ![Notion Image](https://alvys.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/jmo4m9ce/2532418339/c6a9e73916695755f16dce913359/upload_18339483327051100560.png)
6. **Customize Columns:**

   - Just like the Trips Custom Report, you can add, remove, or rearrange columns by clicking the **"Columns"** button.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: What is the difference between "Trips Custom Report" and "Loads Custom Report"?** A: The "Trips Custom Report" focuses on individual trip segments (e.g., a pickup leg or a delivery leg, especially if a load is split into multiple trips), providing granular details about each journey segment. The "Loads Custom Report" provides information about the entire load, consolidating data across all its associated trips.

**Q: Can I save my custom report layouts or filter selections?** A: This article focuses on generating ad-hoc reports. While you can customize columns and filters for each report generation, there is currently no direct "save layout" feature within Custom Reports for future re-use. You would need to re-apply your desired filters and column selections each time you generate the report.
